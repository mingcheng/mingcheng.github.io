# Hello, I'm mingcheng.

I created this page because it is so stressful for my speech to have to reintroduce myself each time I attend community events. I hope this information helps you learn more about who I am 😂.


## Essential Information

Raised in Ningbo, Zhejiang, in 1985. I was hired in 2008 after graduating from Zhejiang University of Science and Technology, and I am currently enrolled at [Shanghai International Studies University (SISU)](https://en.shisu.edu.cn/).

I am currently a PPMC & Committer at the [Apache Software Foundation](https://www.apache.org/) and an open-source enthusiast at [Ant Group](https://github.com/antgroup).

I formerly worked for companies and teams including `Alibaba (Taobao)`, `NetEase`, and `Sifou` as a developer, architect, technical lecturer, technical lead and general manager.

I use Golang, Java, Typescript and other tech stacks in my many years of programming and architecture work (I'm currently learning Rust and Zig). I also have deep experience in the management of technical teams, the development of open source projects, and the promotion of technical communities.
